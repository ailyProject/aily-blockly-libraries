## Description

UNIHIKER K10 music and audio library, supports built-in music, tone playback, TF card recording and playback

## Quick Start

1. Enable `@aily-project/lib-unihiker-k10-music` in Aily Blockly.
2. Add the required music or audio blocks. The generator initializes K10 automatically and initializes the TF card filesystem when recording or TF playback is used.
3. Built-in melodies require no audio assets. Twinkle Star and Jingle Bells are generated as tone sequences for compatibility with older projects.
